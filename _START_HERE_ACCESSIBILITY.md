## 🔴 CRITICAL ISSUES (Fix ASAP)

### 1. Color Contrast ❌
**Current:** `#9898B0` on `#07070D` = 3.8:1 ratio  
**Required:** 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)  
**Impact:** 10% of users can't read secondary text  
**Fix Time:** 30 minutes

### 2. No Skip Links ❌
**Issue:** Keyboard users must tab through 15+ nav items  
**Impact:** All keyboard-only users  
**Fix Time:** 15 minutes

### 3. Missing Semantic HTML ❌
**Issue:** No `<header>`, `<main>`, `<nav>`, `<footer>`  
**Impact:** Screen reader users get lost  
**Fix Time:** 45 minutes

### 4. Icon Buttons Unlabeled ❌
**Issue:** Screen readers announce "button" with no context  
**Impact:** Screen reader users confused  
**Fix Time:** 30 minutes

### 5. No Motion Reduction ❌
**Issue:** Animations cause nausea for some users  
**Impact:** 25% of population (vestibular disorders)  
**Fix Time:** 1 hour

**TOTAL FIX TIME:** ~4 hours → WCAG 2.1 AA compliant ✅
